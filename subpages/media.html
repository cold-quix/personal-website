A video I created as an introduction to the video game Guilty Gear Xrd. The Ground Beneath Our Feet: Our Love-Hate Relationship with Asphalt
My first published article with AJ focuses on an ever-present but rarely discussed topic: asphalt concrete. I am proud to have made a subject which most people see as a symptom of environmental decay into a more nuanced issue which will be relevant no matter what fuel cars use to run.
